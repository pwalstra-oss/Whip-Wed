FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

How much does it cost to rent a Tesla through DriveWhip?
Plans start at $399 per week. Insurance, Tesla-certified maintenance, and unlimited mileage are all included. No hidden fees.
Do I need to own a car to sign up?
No. DriveWhip provides the vehicle. You just need to be approved to drive for Uber or Lyft.
Is insurance included?
Yes. Full insurance coverage is included in your weekly rate. You don't need a separate policy.
Can I drive for multiple platforms?
Yes. DriveWhip Teslas are approved for Uber, Lyft, Instacart, and other gig platforms simultaneously.
Where do I pick up the vehicle in Chicago?
All Chicago pickups are at 2107 W Fulton St, Chicago, IL — in the West Loop neighborhood.
How long does approval take?
Most drivers are approved within 24 hours. Same-day approval is available in many cases.
Do I pay for charging?
Charging costs are significantly lower than gas. Most Chicago DriveWhip drivers charge overnight at home or use public chargers. No gas costs ever.
